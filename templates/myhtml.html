About Me

I am a Computer Science Engineer with a strong passion for both technology and education, with over six years of teaching experience. I specialize in delivering high-quality instruction across a wide range of computer science subjects, including Python, C++, JavaScript, SQL, MongoDB, Pseudocode, logic gates and web development.

I also provide in-depth support for advanced university courses, including Microprocessor-Based System Design (MBSD), Computer Architecture, Computer Organization & Design, Operating Systems, Digital Logic Design, and Database Management Systems (DBMS). I have strong command over low-level programming and architectures, including 8086, 8051, MIPS, and RISC-V, making complex topics easier to understand through step-by-step explanations and practical examples.

My academic background, combined with practical industry experience, allows me to bridge the gap between theory and real-world application.

As a tutor, I follow a hands-on, student-centered teaching approach. I believe learners grasp concepts best when they actively build, experiment, and solve problems. My methodology emphasizes clarity, structured progression, and project-based learning to ensure students can confidently apply what they learn.

I have worked with diverse learners, including school students,IGCSE and A-Level learners, and beginners entering the field of programming. I tailor my lessons according to individual learning styles and academic goals, ensuring consistent progress and strong foundational understanding.

I continuously expand my technical expertise and stay updated with modern tools and technologies. My goal is to empower students with the skills, confidence, and problem-solving mindset needed to succeed in the evolving field of computer science.
